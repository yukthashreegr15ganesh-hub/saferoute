The Problem

  • Traditional maps optimize for speed, not safety.
  • Emergency tools are often buried in submenus when seconds matter.
  • Lack of real-time insights on street lighting, CCTV, and crowds.

The SafeRoute Solution

A comprehensive ecosystem prioritizing personal security over transit time.

Intelligent Routing

Routes evaluated on lighting, CCTV density, and historical safety scores, categorized into SAFE, MODERATE, or DANGER.

Guardian SOS System

1-tap broadcast. Real-time location sharing, stealth mode triggers, and audio recording to emergency contacts.

ARIA (AI Co-Pilot)

Powered by Claude. A conversational AI assistant that scans zones, plans escapes, and schedules safety check-ins.

Sentinel Network

Crowdsourced incident reports and peer-to-peer "Shadow Walk" for live tracking by trusted friends.
